Shadow-reset simulator

Replays every one of your DUPR matches since a cutoff date (default 2024-04-16, the start of the last public ratings overhaul) with your reliability forced to 0%. That gives the maximum per-match impact the model can assign — it's a what-if lower bound for "what would my rating be if DUPR rebuilt it from scratch tomorrow?"

This uses a reverse-engineered model, not official DUPR code. Treat the output as a directional what-if estimate — the shape of the curve matters more than the absolute number.
